About

Shital's Kitchen

Shital's Kitchen is the most significant player in the food delivery market. The company was created in Maharastra by Shital Suresh Kasar, an internet holding company, in 2022. Now this food delivery platform includes 3,000 restaurants in more than 1 state. The platform consists of a large variety of cuisines and different dishes together with safe and trouble-free payment options. To make the ordering process even more convenient, Shital's Kitchen offers users a cross-platform alternative.

Our Mission

Our primary mission is to provide food at lower cost and have some happy customers. As we have came so far in just some months we had a goal to includes 30,000 restaurants with us and to lauch our company throughout the country.

Focus

Our focus is to get more and more happy and satisfied customers. Our Employees work 24*7 to make it successful by dealing with the best restaurants around the country. We have our best help support team that monitor every customer review and work on it. We never ever fail to reach to anyone even at night.
